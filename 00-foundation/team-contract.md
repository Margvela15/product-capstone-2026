## Decision-Making

- **Day-to-day decisions:** Any two members may decide minor formatting, tool choice, or minor scope questions.  
- **Product direction:** All four discuss; simple majority decides. Archil holds the tiebreaker if needed.  
- **Major pivots:** Require a dedicated meeting ≥30 minutes, written note committed within 24 hours, majority agreement. Decisions never made over Messenger.  
- **Documentation:** All product-direction decisions logged in `decisions/decision-log.md` (folder created Week 3).

## Conflict Resolution

**KIU 4-Step Conflict Protocol:**

1. **Verbal Discussion (15 min):** Each party states their position once. Interrupting is not allowed.  
2. **Written View (10 min):** Each writes a brief paragraph summarizing position. Shared with all. Giorgi Papidze facilitates if needed.  
3. **Cool-off (24 hrs):** No decision; written views stay visible.  
4. **Program Lead Decision:** If unresolved, Archil makes final call. Dissenting view can be logged but does not block execution.  

- **Escalation:** If Archil is involved or protocol fails twice, any member may contact zeshan.ahmad@kiu.edu.ge.
